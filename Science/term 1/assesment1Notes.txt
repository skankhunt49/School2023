Trophic levels

types of organisms, their role in terms of food
  autotrophs, make their own food, producers
  heterotrophs, switch(diet) :
                  diet == consumes plants :
                    herbivore
                  diet == eat animals which eat plants :
                    carnivore
                  diet == eat plants and animals :
                    omnivore
                  diet == eat fruits :
                    frugivore
  decomposers, extract energy from dead things
  
photosynthesis = water + co2 == glucose + oxygen when subject to chlorophyll and sunlight[uv]
