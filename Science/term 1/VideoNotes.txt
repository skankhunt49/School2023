all organisms need nutrients, space, water and possibly a mate

all ecosystems have competition, symbiosis and predator and prey relationships

intraspecific competition is within one species
interspecific competition is between different species

symbiosis is a close relation between two or more organisms

Types
commensalism definition : one organism benefits other remains same
mutualism definition : both benefits
parasitism definition : one benefits at others expense

Mutualism examples
microbiome of humans
termites eat wood and single celled protea digest interact
cleaner fish and sharks

Commensalism examples
hitch hiker mites
orchids grow on tree branches
snails and trees

Parasitism examples
lampreys attach and feed on other fish
liver fluke attaches to your liver and feeds on it
tapeworm
